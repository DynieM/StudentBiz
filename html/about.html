About StudentBiz

Our platform allows any verified student to create a profile spotlighting
their business offerings, from creative services to tech solutions
and everything in between. Whether you're a freelancer, sole
proprietor, or startup founder, StudentBiz gives you the tools and
exposure needed to convert your campus side-hustle into a
full-fledged enterprise.
